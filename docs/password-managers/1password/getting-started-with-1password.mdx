# Getting Started with 1Password

:::tip Ready for the next step?
If you've been using your phone's built-in password manager or your browser's password manager, you're already ahead of most people. 1Password takes things further with a polished experience, excellent family sharing, and premium features designed for ease of use.
:::

1Password is a **premium**, **polished**, and **secure** password manager trusted by millions of individuals and businesses. It's known for its intuitive design, excellent cross-platform support, and powerful features that make password management effortless.

---

## Why Choose 1Password?

- **Watchtower Security Alerts**: 1Password monitors your saved passwords and alerts you if any are weak, reused, or compromised in known data breaches ([Learn More](https://support.1password.com/watchtower/)).

- **Travel Mode**: Hide sensitive vaults when crossing borders and restore them with a click when you arrive safely ([Learn More](https://support.1password.com/travel-mode/)).

- **Cross-Platform Support**: Access your vault on Windows, macOS, Linux, Android, iOS, and through browser extensions for all major browsers ([Learn More](https://1password.com/downloads/)).

- **Family & Team Plans**: Share passwords securely with family members or colleagues, with fine-grained access controls ([Learn More](https://1password.com/families/)).

- **Passkey Support**: 1Password supports passkeys, the modern replacement for passwords that's more secure and easier to use ([Learn More](https://1password.com/product/passkeys)).

---

## Key Features at a Glance

### 1. **Secure Vault**

Store unlimited logins, credit cards, secure notes, documents, and more in an encrypted vault. Your data is protected with AES-256 encryption and a Secret Key that adds an extra layer of security beyond your master password.

### 2. **Password Generator**

Create strong, unique passwords with customizable length and character options. 1Password can generate memorable passphrases too ([Learn More](https://1password.com/password-generator/)).

### 3. **Auto-Fill and Auto-Save**

1Password integrates with your browser and mobile devices to automatically fill login credentials, credit cards, and addresses. It also offers to save new logins as you create them.

### 4. **Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)**

1Password can store your TOTP (time-based one-time password) codes and auto-fill them alongside your login credentials, streamlining the 2FA process ([Learn More](https://support.1password.com/one-time-passwords/)).

### 5. **Secure Sharing**

Share credentials with family members, team members, or anyone—even people who don't use 1Password—using secure, expiring links ([Learn More](https://support.1password.com/share-items/)).

## Getting Started with 1Password

1. **Sign Up**: Visit [1password.com](https://1password.com) and create your account.
2. **Save Your Emergency Kit**: 1Password provides an Emergency Kit with your Secret Key. Store it somewhere safe—you'll need it if you ever lose access to your account.
3. **Set a Strong Master Password**: Choose a memorable but strong password. This is the only password you'll need to remember.
4. **Install 1Password**: Download the apps on your devices and install browser extensions.
5. **Import Your Passwords**: Use the built-in import tool to bring passwords from browsers, other password managers, or CSV files.
6. **Enable Two-Factor Authentication**: For added security, enable 2FA in your account settings.
